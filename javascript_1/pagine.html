Basic Concepts

Addition: Combining quantities. Example: 2 + 3 = 5.

Subtraction: Finding the difference between quantities. Example: 5 - 2 = 3.

Multiplication: Grouping quantities into equal parts. Example: 3 × 4 = 12.

Division: Splitting a quantity into equal parts. Example: 12 ÷ 3 = 4.
